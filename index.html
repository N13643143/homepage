I am a postdoc at Virginia Tech (mentor: Leonardo Mihalcea). I did
my PhD at Rutgers University from 2017 to 2022. My thesis was
advised by Anders Buch. I am currently on the job market.
Here is my CV.

While I am broadly interested in combinatorial algebraic geometry,
my research has centered around the geometry of (generalized)
flag varieties, and in particular, quantum Schubert calculus and
characterizations of geometric properties via the combinatorics of
root systems.

My email address is weihong@vt.edu. Please note that I no
longer have access to my old math.rutgers email.
